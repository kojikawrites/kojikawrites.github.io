# Future Post from Christmas 2099

This is a **draft post** dated December 25, 2099 - far in the future! This post demonstrates how draft posts work in the system.

## About Draft Posts

Draft posts are stored in the `_drafts` subdirectory and are:

- ✅ **Visible in development mode** - You can preview and edit them
- ❌ **Hidden in production** - They won't appear on the live site
- 📅 **Can have any date** - Including future dates like this one!

## Why Use Drafts?

Drafts are useful for:

1. **Work in progress** - Posts you're still writing
2. **Future scheduling** - Content planned for later (though this system doesn't auto-publish)
3. **Ideas** - Rough drafts and brainstorming
4. **Testing** - Experimental content you don't want public yet
